Atlas Global Bank
The Scenario

The Data Science team updates 20GB of weights inside a 500GB Fraud ML Model. A Kafka stream triggers the APAC Airflow pipeline.

THE FLEXCACHE ADVANTAGE
Removing the Sync Burden

Instead of burdening Airflow with a custom `aws s3 cp` task, FlexCache handles movement invisibly at the 4KB block level. 480GB of unchanged blocks are served instantly locally. Only the 20GB of changed blocks stream across the WAN, dropping egress costs from $10.00 to $0.40 per run and eliminating orchestrator wait times.

Anti-Pattern: Orchestrator-Managed Full Copies

The cost of forcing your workflow tool to also manage massive data synchronization.

High Management Burden
The Scenario

The Data Science team updates 20GB of weights inside a 500GB Fraud ML Model. A Kafka stream triggers the APAC Airflow pipeline.

THE IMMUTABILITY BOTTLENECK
The Traditional Flaw (Cost & Effort)

Because object storage (like S3) treats files as immutable blobs, updating 20GB of weights replaces the entire 500GB file. Without an edge data fabric, the Airflow pipeline is forced to execute a custom `aws s3 cp` to pull the entire 500GB object across the WAN. This incurs $10.00 in egress fees per run, stalls the pipeline, and burdens engineers with maintaining sync logic.
